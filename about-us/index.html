About Us

FLPNO.com is an online magazine aimed at bringing you the latest news from and about Filipino-Americans. This includes people you may not have known were of Filipino descent and Filipino-Americans you may not have heard of but should know. For a longer explanation of why this site was started, check out thispost.

If you have news or events you’d like to share, or if you’d like to just drop us a line and let us know how we’re doing, you can reach us via e-mail at editors@flpno.com or by mail at: FLPNO, P.O. Box 6038, Folsom, CA 95763. You can also find us on Twitter@FLPNO, or onFacebook.

Mabuhay!
